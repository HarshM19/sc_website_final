Event Name : Anti-Tobacco Poster Making competition (Digital)

Event Date : 11 March 2023

Event Description : Awareness campaign was organized at this institute on various activities for the students and faculty during 11 March 2023. Tobacco and drug addiction is major cause to ruin the health and mind of every person. To motivate all VESITIANS towards healthy and addiction free life under the campaign “ Jindagi Chuno, Nasha Nahi”
"जिंदगी चुनिए। नशा नहीं। "
The oath campaign was organized and all students and faculty members were asked to submit their acceptance of healthy life and keep themselves away from Addiction in any form. The VESIT Community was asked to provide their support by signing the Pledge to confirm their support for the No-Tobacco campaign. Here is the overall summary of the responses that were gathered from the google Form. The Oath was circulated in Hindi , Marathi and in English I confirm Herewith that I will definitely help in making VESIT a No-Tobacco Zone. I will help people who are addicted to tobacco to quit Tobacco consumption. I also assure that I will not consume tobacco in any form,
