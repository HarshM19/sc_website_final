VESIT STUDENTS CLUB

Event Name : Meraki(Wall Painting)

Event Date - 11 Feb 2023

Event Description :On 11th February, a wall painting competition was jointly organised by the VESIT Student's Club and Social Responsibility Team (SoRT) in collaboration with the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation G- North Ward (BMC). The event was held at Chaityabhoomi, Dadar Chowpatty, and commenced at 8 am. A total of 22 walls were painted by enthusiastic VESIT students, who displayed remarkable creativity and artistic skills. The theme of the competition was ‘Nurture Nature’ and participants were required to draw something related to the theme. The students' enthusiastic participation and excitement created a vibrant and lively atmosphere, which was truly inspiring for all those present. The esteemed judge for the competition was Mr Gaurav Bhatkar, who evaluated the entries based on their originality, creativity, and adherence to the theme. The faculty members present at the event included Mrs Manisha Joshi, Mrs Saylee Gharge, and Mr Ajinkya Valanjoo, who provided valuable guidance and support to the students throughout the competition. To acknowledge the outstanding performances, a cash prize pool of INR 6000 was distributed among the top three winning teams. The event was a resounding success and served as a platform for the VESIT students to showcase their talent and express their commitment to the cause of environmental conservation.
