VESIT STUDENTS CLUB

Event Name : Drops of Hope (Blood Donation)

Event Date - 22 Feb 2023

Event Description :On February 22nd, 2023, the Student's Club in collaboration with SoRT VESIT organized a blood donation drive called "Drops of Hope". The drive took place in the Drawing hall of VESIT and was open to all VES students. In addition to the blood donation drive, Thalassemia Test and Eye check-up were also organized. The drive ran from 10:30 am to 4 pm and saw 226 students register for blood donation. Out of these, 118 students were eligible to donate blood. Refreshments such as Glucon-d, biscuits, and bananas were provided to those who donated blood. A team of 8-10 doctors was responsible for the blood collection drive, and the collected blood was sent to JJ Blood Bank. To raise awareness about the drive, the SoRT Aesthetics team decorated the college entrance, and a photo booth was set up. Thanks to everyone's willing participation and cooperation, the blood donation drive was a success and helped save numerous lives.
